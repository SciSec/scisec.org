Conference Site:Beijing Institute of Technology International Exchange Center

Introduction: The Beijing Institute of Technology International Exchange Center is located in the core area of Zhongguancun, Haidian District. This is a foreign exchange and service organization with over 30 years of history. It is an important window and link for external exchange and cooperation of Beijing Institute of Technology. It is close to landmarks and universities such as Suzhou Bridge, Zhongguancun, and Beijing Foreign Studies University. The communication center has an elegant environment and convenient transportation, making it an ideal place for academic exchanges, teacher-student visits, and international conferences. The exchange center provides related services such as exchange project management, foreign affairs services, reception of international students, and conference support.
