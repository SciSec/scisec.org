How to get here

NTNU in Gjøvik is situated on Kallerud, about 20-minute walk from the bus and train station in Gjøvik and less than 10 minutes away when taking the bus.

Buses and train

Travel planner - Entur

Travel from Oslo airport:

  • If you wish to use a rental car the fastest route is along road E16 towards Roa and then along road 4 to Gjøvik. This route will take approx. 1,5 hours.
  • Alternative route is train to Moelv station and bus or taxi from here. Prebook Taxi at Moelv Taxi : +47 62 36 72 79

City bus in Gjøvik:

  • The buses B42, B44, B45, B46 and B47 stop by the university.
