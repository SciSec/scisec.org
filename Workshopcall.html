Workshop Call for Papers

(1) AI Crypto and Security Workshop (AI-CryptoSec)
What is about: This AI-CryptoSec workshop seeks to explore new ideas on AI-based cryptographic science and security engineering, as well as broader strategic, ethical, and policy aspects of security-critical AI-based systems. This workshop is held in cooperation with JSAI SIG-SEC.

(2) Theory and Application of Blockchain and NFT Workshop (TA-BC-NFT)
What is about: This TA-BC-NFT workshop seeks to explore new ideas on science of Blockchain and Non-Fungible Tokens, as well as broader strategic, engineering and policy aspects of Distributed including Digital Currency.
(3) Mathematical Science of Quantum Safety and its Application Workshop (MathSci-Qsafe)
What is about: This MathSci-Qsafe workshop seeks to explore new ideas on science on Quantum safety including Quantum communication and Post-Quantum Cryptography and its application to social systems as well as broader science and engineering aspects of long time security and privacy.
