Registration

The 8th International Conference on Science of Cyber Security (SciSec 2026) will be held in the Beijing Institute of Technology International Exchange Center, from May 29 to May 31, 2026. Anyone related to the area of cybersecurity are encouraged to register for the conference as soon as possible. Note that for each accepted paper, at least one author should register before May 15, 2026.

Registration Notes

  • The standard registration fee includes admission to all technical sessions, and all social functions as well as a copy of the pre-proceeding and a banquet. Note that the conference extra proceeding and tutorial are NOT included in the registration.
  • If one or more copies of the conference extra proceedings are needed, please indicate the number of copies and pay the extra fees for them. Detailed post addresses are needed for delivering the extra proceedings after they become available.
  • Note that for each accepted paper, at least one author should register the conference, and present their paper at the conference in order to have their paper included in the conference proceeding.
